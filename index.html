About :

Zugzwang Chess Club is a community of chess enthusiasts who come together to play, learn, and share their love of the game. Our club is open to players of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to experienced players. We offer a variety of activities and events, including tournaments, workshops, and social events. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone can enjoy the game of chess. So come join us and let's play some chess!

Origin Story:

The Zugzwang Chess Club was founded in Grade 11 by Sarveshwar Senthil Kumar and Liam Pham. We were frustrated by the mismanagement and lack of structure in their school's official chess club. Determined to create a space for passionate players, we established the club, named after the chess term for a position where any move worsens the situation, symbolizing their drive to always improve. Despite facing resistance and being initially marginalized by the school's existing chess leadership, We persisted, building a thriving community that emphasized learning, competition, and a love for the game. Their efforts eventually led to the decline of the old club and the rise of Zugzwang as a respected force in the school's chess scene.
